    Covid Situation

    Yemen had its first recorded case on April 10th 2020. Soon after, cases were spreading across the country like hot cakes. It was safe to predict that most of Yemen was going to drastically suffer due to the inability of international aid to enter the country easily. Currently, Yemen has about 2,000 active cases with about 1,000 recoveries and 500 deaths. It is also predicted that about half of Yemen's population will eventually contract COVID-19. Also most Toll passes to many provinces only have about 1 or 2 digital thermometers, and that too with hardly any spare batteries.
